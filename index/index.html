Spanish Omelette

  1. 2 plates of spanish omelette
  2. 7 min. prep time
  3. 7 min.cook time
  4. 14 min total
  5. 2 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Olive oil
  • 2 Medium eggs
  • Coriander
  • 1 Bulb onion/spring
  • Capsicum
  • Pinch of salt
  • Powdered ginger

PROCEDURE

1. Pre heat the pan
2. Finely chop onion, coriander,capsicum and place in a bowl
2. Place broken eggs in a separate bowl and whisk
3. Mix the contents of the 2 bowls together using a folk
4. Add olive oil to the pan
5. Spread a portion of the mixture on the pan and allow to cook for a few minutes
6. Flip to the other side and allow to cook
7. Serve the omelette on a plate
8. Add olive oil to the pan and spread the remaining content to cook
9. Flip to the other side, you can add a teaspoon of olive oil and cook till golden brown
10.Serve while hot
